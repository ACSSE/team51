About

Bursify was soley created to address one of the biggest problems facing South Africa today - Student Funding.
The #FeesMustFall movement of 2016 highlighted the extent to which this lack of higher education funding is widespread in South Africa. Our main goal is to connect students with corporate companies that are seeking to sponsor the countries top students by making the application process seamless.
We also provide students a platform to fund raise for various projects through the community of sponsors.
Bursify is a South African solution to a South African problem.
